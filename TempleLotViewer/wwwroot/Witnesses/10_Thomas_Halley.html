Page 366 Thomas Halley of lawful age being produced, sworn and examined on the part of the complaints, testified as follows, - Direct examination by Mr Kelley, - 1 Where do you live Mr Halley? Here in Independence I lived up here in this Mrs Morgan Block for five years, and down where I live now for five years, and that will make ten years. 2 You have lived here ten years? Yes sir. 3 Are these places where you have lived within the corporate limits of the City of Independence? Yes sir, and I came here ten years ago this spring. 4 Do you know the piece of property called the temple lot that is in controversy in this case? Yes sir. 5 How long have you known it? I have known it ever since I have been down here. 6 And you knew it ten years ago? Yes sir. 7 Was it fenced at the time you came here? It was not fenced until the summer of 1883, - I told Mr Kelley it was fenced in 1884 but I was mistaken in that, for it was fenced the year after I came here which was it was fenced the year after I came here which was in 1882 so it was fenced in 1883. It was fenced in the summer of 1883 some time but I don't think there was any trees put on it until in the fall of that year. Now that is the best of my recollection about it. I know the time it was fenced for the old gentleman that fenced it was living up at our house for four years, and I was glad to see it fenced, for it lay there rough and tumble as a loose lot, and was used as a camping ground for circuses, and for a circus ground or any other purpose that people who came along wanted to use it for. 8 Was there any improvements put on it before you came here? No sir, none at all. 9 Was there any improvements on it when you came here No sir, - that is what I said. 10 Was there any improvements put on it before it was fenced, - before   Page 367 11 If there had been any improvements you would have seen it? Yes sir. Cross examination by Mr Southern, - 12 Are you a member of the Reorganized church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints? I have been a member of the church ever sine 1846, - that is I was a member of the old church at first, and I have been a member of the reorganized church about seventeen years, as near as I can get at it. I could tell you just how long I have been a member of the reorganized church if I had known you wanted it. _________________________ Sworn to before me on the day, within the hours, and at the place in that behalf aforesaid, and subscribed in my presence this gay of 1892. _________________________ Notary Public.
